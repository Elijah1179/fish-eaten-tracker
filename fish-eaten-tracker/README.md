\# Fish Eaten Tracker



A RuneLite Plugin Hub plugin that tracks how many fish you eat, including Chambers of Xeric fish.



\## Features



\- Tracks standard edible fish

\- Tracks Chambers of Xeric fish

\- Overlay showing total fish eaten

\- Reset button in config

\- Saves total per RuneLite profile



\## Included Chambers fish



\- Pysk fish

\- Suphi fish

\- Leckish fish

\- Brawk fish

\- Mycil fish

\- Roqed fish

\- Kyren fish
